Treasure Chest Cave

Open chests to obtain rewards and exclusive cards. This page summarizes the Treasure Chest Cave system (adapted from the CWK-Revived Fandom wiki).

Overview

The Treasure Chest Cave provides players with two main types of chests with differing costs and rewards. Use bones or gems to open chests and try your luck for better odds.

Standard Chest

Cost: 50 Bones

  • Cool: 90% chance
  • Rare: 45% chance
  • Algebraic: 15% chance

Standard chests provide a balanced variety of items and are the most affordable option.

Premium Chest

Cost: 200 Gems

  • Cool: 90% chance
  • Rare: 60% chance
  • Algebraic: 25% chance

Premium chests cost gems but have better chances for rare or algebraic items.

Tips & Notes

  • Premium chests usually give a higher chance for rare and algebraic items.
  • Check the in-game store and events for limited-time chests and discounts.
  • Drop chances may reflect item rarity tiers; the numerical values are taken from the community page and may change with updates.
